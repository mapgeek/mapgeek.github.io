Fusion SEO Announces Expansion with SOCii Media Marketing

Fusion SEO, a private company in the Digital Marketing Services sector, announced today that it continues to expand its services with the merger of SOCii Media Marketing. Fusion SEO with SOCii’s expertise will continue to reinforce the company’s growth with digital strategy consulting, channel partners, as well as develop and enhance the visibility of its digital services in the areas such as Technology, Pharma, Healthcare, Dental, Law, Finance, Manufacturing, Logistics, Professional Services, Building Development, Luxury & Retail, and Government.

CathyAnn Sarra, SOCii’s Principal & Managing Partner, is excited to be apart of the team as Chief Relationship Officer, for Fusion SEO. Sarra states, “I appreciate Carl Mancuso, Managing Principal, including my team and me with Fusion SEO. It has been a seamless transition, and we look forward to strengthening our business development and delivering information about our services,” Sarra said. She states, “We will ensure our company services will meet the demands for innovation, interactive, relevant content that is cost-effective for our clients. “We are excited to continue working with industry experts, and we look forward to partnering with clients who are looking for a trusted Digital Marketing firm.” Sarra goes on to state, “I appreciate the opportunity to be apart of Fusion SEO’s growth and continued success within this new and emerging digital agency ecosystem.”

Carl Mancuso, Managing Principal, states, “We know as a small, agile Digital Marketing Agency, we need to be on the front line with a forward-thinking approach for our clients, and we believe in Fusion SEO’s ability to execute this business model.” Mancuso and Sarra see their services as great assets for enterprise projects, as well as medium-sized and small business alike. Mancuso states, “It is an exciting time for us at Fusion SEO as we look forward to our future and welcome CathyAnn Sarra and the SOCii team!”
