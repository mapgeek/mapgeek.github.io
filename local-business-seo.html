SEO Services

Local business SEO shares a lot in common with other brands or kinds of SEO. But it also is unique in many ways, and therefore requires a very different approach than national, enterprise, or ecommerce SEO solutions. One of the biggest differences with local business SEO is what’s called “geo-targeting”.

Geo-targeting is simply identifying a target market, geographically, for your local business. Then, you look at combining the services you offer with the names of the towns, cities, counties, states that you do business in, or that you consider to be your target market. That’s the formula.

Specific Geo-Targeting:

Let’s look at an example – let’s say you are a cosmetic surgeon in Montgomery County, Maryland. And let’s go further and say your practice is in the city of Bethesda, Maryland. What makes the best business sense is to geo-target your website so that people who are looking for a cosmetic surgeon in your area are the ones who will land on your website. Think about it — it wouldn’t make much sense to have people who are looking for a cosmetic surgeon in Delaware landing on your site. Instead, you want people in Bethesda, MD, and surrounds to land on your website and find your practice. By attracting that highly targeted, quality traffic in your market, you increase your conversions and can ultimately increase your bottom-line.

The process whereby someone searches for something on a search engine, arrives at your website and makes an appointment, purchase, etc… is known as a conversion. Effective local business SEO is really therefore about facilitating and increasing conversions over time through increased exposure in the search engines within your target market.
